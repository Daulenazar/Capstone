First travel in the ship:

Of course, no trip was complete without tasting delicious treats! The girls loved trying different flavors of ice cream—coconut, mango, and even a strange but tasty purple one. Every evening, they strolled through the busy streets, their hands sticky from melted ice cream, looking at the bright lights and street performances. The markets were full of souvenirs, and the girls each picked a small, handmade elephant figurine to take home.
